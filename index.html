Hi, I’m a CS senior undergraduate at School of Information and Technology at ShanghaiTech University. In Fall 2023, I was an exchange student at University of Berkeley, University.

My research focuses on Human-Centered Computing, with the goal of understanding and modeling human behavior and societal dynamics influenced by technology. I employ a mix of methods, such as interviews and statistical analysis, to explore these dynamics from various perspectives, including industrial structures and social media. Ultimately, I aim to contribute to the development of technology that positively impacts society. At ShanghaiTech, I am a member of the ViSeer Lab under the supervision of Prof. Quan Li, where I actively participate in research projects on HCI, Visual Analytics, and Social Computing.

I am also an crazy fan on histroy and strategy games like Paradox, enjoying Europa Universalis IV!
